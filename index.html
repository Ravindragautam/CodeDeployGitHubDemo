Experience nature at its best. Plan a visit to the hills and mountains

The world has amazing hills and mountains destinations. Some of these offer an ideal setting for a romantic getaway, while others are just great for an enjoyable family vacation. Relax and soaking in the expansive views, take a stroll through lush tea gardens or learn more about the culture, destinations in the hills offer something for everyone. To be honest, there's nothing quite like standing at the top of a mountain, with the clouds and the rest of the world stretched out below you and inhaling the fresh mountain. The excitement of completing a challenging climb, the camaraderie of your friends, the knowledge that you've done something that few other people do and the feeling that you're genuinely at the top of the world. Nothing can beat that feeling. It's no wonder that once people start climbing mountains, they never want to stop! Though undeniably exciting, mountain climbing trips require a level of advanced preparation and physical fitness that few other types of travel can equal. Below, you'll find a primer on the basics that you'll need to consider before you embark on your trip. Whether you're planning to climb the Rockies, the Himalayas or any other mountain range, know that an incredible adventure awaits you.

What Makes Hill Stations the Unrivalled Vacation Getaway

Think of a relaxing vacation amidst natural beauty and you would visualise a visit to one of the hill stations. The splendid beauty that nature has given mountains is unmatchable. And we get to witness this loveliness in hill stations. There are multiple reasons why a visit to a hill station is an attraction for tourists. The scenic surroundings of hill stations hold peaceful serenity and provide adventure sports that not only entertain you but also provide engaging activities in clean, breathable air. To top it, hill stations in India have the power to remain as golden memories that you savour for years. If you have a time crunch and are looking for a quick trip, search for ‘hill station near me’. You will have a plethora of options for hill stations within your state or the neighbouring states.


Best Hills Stations in the World

Srinagar, India

Kashmir, aptly known as “Paradise on Earth”, houses the mesmerising capital city of Srinagar. Srinagar is marked as the best hill station in India by many tourists. Snuggled on the banks of the Jhelum River, it has the famed Dal Lake as its major attraction. Staying in houseboats, watching floating markets, floating gardens, shopping on the shikaras (boats) or just chilling on the side of the lake could be your activities. There are Mughal gardens, monuments and buildings to visit while you enjoy the cool, crisp weather. Enjoy folk dance, buy local artefacts and sample Kashmiri cuisine to complete your experience of this queen of all hill stations.


Darjeeling, India

Looking for hill stations with a difference? Visit Darjeeling, the British holiday spot nestled within world-famous tea gardens. Situated in the state of West Bengal, it is located at the foothills of the Himalayas. You would want to trek up to Tiger Hill to witness the first rays of the dawn reflected on the Kanchenjunga peak. Another unique experience would be to ride on the UNESCO-certified Toy Train to the Batasia Loop. Darjeeling is a must-visit destination among the hill stations in India.


Manali, India

Gazing up to the majestic Himalayas, Manali is one of the top hill stations in India. This picturesque tourist spot is equally popular with backpackers, honeymooners and luxury vacationers. Its engaging charm makes many domestic and international tourists consider it the best hill station in India. Go for rafting, skiing and paragliding or trek up to Rohtang Pass to enjoy the spellbinding glory of glaciers and peaks. With the town peppered with temples and monasteries, Manali offers a spiritual vibe. For energising tours to hill stations near Delhi, book one trip to Manali and gather memories of a lifetime.


Nainital, India

Here is a tourist spot that is a combination of tradition and natural charm. A popular retreat for the British, Nainital is one of the most-visited hill stations in Uttarakhand. It is a location scattered with places to visit. You have pristine lakes and imposing peaks along with temples and monuments to see. Love adventures on your trips to hill stations? Indulge in camping, horse riding, rafting, paragliding, boating, trekking and others. Don’t forget to go for a wildlife safari in the famous Jim Corbett National Park. Besides adventure activities, Nainital is also a religious location for you to soak in some spiritualism.


Ooty, India

Ooty is one of the British-favoured, pre-independence hill stations. A hill retreat with its thrilling Toy Train, Ooty hill station still retains its old-world charm. The lakes, gardens, museums, churches and quaint colonial cottages are some of the soothing attractions Ooty offers to its visitors. You would love to visit the tea gardens with about 600 varieties of flowers and treat yourself to its many activities apt for a hill station. Ooty has a comforting, laid-back atmosphere that is great for a traveller looking to destress in restful tranquillity. Do not forget to try the variety of cuisines here.


Munnar, India

Munnar is not only one of the best hill stations in Kerala but also a favoured pan-Indian hill retreat. If your idea of hill stations is fluffy white mountains and cold, crisp air, think again. The undulating valleys and meadows of Munnar, covered in tea and coffee plantations, will make you forget snow-covered peaks. Throw in the Chinnakanal waterfalls, the Pallivasal falls, Mattupetty Lake, Eravikulam National Park and trekking to Anamudi peak – and you have a complete holiday package in Munnar. This destination will provide you with both a peaceful retreat and eye-pleasing scenery.


Innsbruck, Austria

This enchanting city with brightly coloured houses complements the sparkling snow from the surrounding Alpine peaks. The lovely surroundings make it a perfect mountain destination for tourists searching for gorgeousness. Innsbruck, which is a celebrated international ski destination, has much more to offer. Visit the Ambras Castle, Imperial Palace, St James Cathedral, Swaroski Crystal World and the Alpine Zoo. Indulge in ski jump and trekking to elevate your experience in one of the remarkable hill stations. The unusual activities and the mind-blowing surroundings make this location memorable for tourists.


Fussen, Germany

This is a dreamland of hill stations filled with an atmosphere celebrating history and heritage. Located in the Bavarian area, it has all the elements of a snowy retreat. With castles and mountains towering all around, your stay is bound to be peaceful. Besides the usual activities of mountain biking, trekking and hiking, get involved in violin and lute music. Fussen is famous for violin making. Fussen is only a half-an-hour walk from Austria in case you make a last-minute plan to visit Austria.


Wanaka, New Zealand

This picture-perfect getaway down south is worth a visit of a lifetime. If you are looking for a vacation in hill stations that offer a glimpse into its historical origins, this is the place. Wanaka is one of the few places that still hold on to the Maori tribal culture. The ethnic culture adds to the vacation vibes when you visit this travel destination. In a very scenic landscape, you get to enjoy all kinds of activities such as cycling downhill, snowboarding and diving out of a plane. Enjoy a different time zone with its special feel in one of the most charming places in the world.


Levi, Finland

Want to have a fairytale location for your holiday? Almost nothing can beat the out-of-the-world surroundings of this mountain destination. Levi offers a lot more than most other hill stations. Not only can you enjoy budget skiing options, you can witness the magic of the Northern Lights, also called the Aurora Borealis. Some other astounding affairs would be staying in a lavish igloo and enjoying a reindeer safari. Visit Levi to take your hill station experience to an elevated level.


Hill stations are one of the best choices for you if you are looking for a retreat from your busy life to rejuvenate. Whether you take your trip as a backpacker or enjoy adventure sports with the entire family, a visit to a hill station is recommended for better mental and physical health. All you have to do is type ‘hill station near me’ on your mobile and have all information at your fingertips. Go ahead and take the plunge.


FAQs - Frequently Asked Questions

What are the best hills and mountain destinations for a trip?

There are many interesting destinations nestled in the hills and mountains but some places you should definitely check out include, Nanda Devi, Pahalgam, Mussoorie situated in Northern India and Kodaikanal, Munnar set in the lush Western Ghats, amongst others! These places offer stunning views and lots of fun activities to indulge in.

What are the best things to do on a trip to the hills and mountain?

Choose between various kinds of fun and adventurous activities when visiting a destination situated on a hill and or a mountain top. These include: trekking, rock climbing, hiking, camping, paragliding, river rafting and more, apart from exploring the scenic beauty of the place and spending time to make some memories and clicking pictures.

Why is a hills and mountain trip a good idea?

Visiting a place located on a hill or a mountain can be a great idea not only because of the beautiful scenery and clean air, but also because it makes way for you to explore the local culture and traditions. You can choose between various fun and adventurous activities like hiking, trekking and camping.
